In order to apply for tribal membership in the Wicocomico Indian Nation, the following steps are required:

  1. Have your ancestor lineage as far back as possible to the Wicocomico, William Taptico or other proven family of the Wicocomico.
  2. List the reference's where you gathered your information.
  3. Complete the application, it can be copied from our web site.
  4. Sign the Wicocomico Creed, that will be sent to you after receipt of application.
  5. Forward to the address listed below.
  6. Your application will be reviewed and researched to determine if it's correct.
  7. If approved, you will be placed on the Tribal Membership Rolls.
