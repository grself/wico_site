    THE POWHATAN LANGUAGE IS EXTINCT! Currently approximately 1000 words of the Eastern Algonquian language (200 plus are Powhatan) are known.William Strachey, while in Virginia between 1610 and 1611, recorded the language and attempted to record the sounds using the english alphabet.Capt John Smith recorded several hundred during his exploration, that is the extent of the language.
